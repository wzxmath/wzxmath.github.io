Workshop on p-adic Modular Forms and the Fontaine–Mazur Conjecture

Dates: July 10–11, 2026
Location: School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Science and Technology of China, Hefei, Anhui, China

The aim of this workshop is to bring together researchers working in the areas of p-adic modular forms and Galois representations.
